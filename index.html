Nail Art Salon strives to give quality service to our beloved customers. We offer a deluxe service for manicures and pedicures, including a complimentary drink, hot towel, and massage. Customers can choose regular polish, powder dip, or gel polish for all services. For our acrylic services, we offer removals, fill-in, or a complete full set. Additionally, we offer waxing services for eyebrows, chin, lips, and full face.

We are a walk-ins only salon. Appointments are not offered or required.

Your Satisfaction is Our Priority

Keeping our commitment to cleanliness, safety, and hygiene, we sterilize and sanitize our equipment for all services and treatments. Your health and safety are our utmost priorities. All pedicure liners are disposable to prevent the spread of bacteria, fungus, and infectious diseases. All our files and buffers will be discarded after being used on each customer.

Thank you for choosing us as your place for hand, feet, and beauty care.
